About Us

This is a website developed by Team-HN-G01-JavaStudio-2025A from RMIT University, a team of dedicated students who are passionate about digital solutions for social impact. As part of our academic journey, we've developed this website to support awareness and understanding of Australia's Closing the Gap initiative. Our website provides:

  • Visualisation and comparison of data about age, health and education from the 2016 and 2021 Australian Censuses
  • State/territory-level breakdowns for region-specific analysis
  • Accessible dashboards designed for a wide range of users

Our Purpose

  • Our mission is to support the Closing the Gap initiative by focusing on two key outcomes:
Outcome 1

Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander people enjoy long and healthy lives

Outcome 5

Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander students achieve their full learning potential

  • To achieve this, our platform aims to:
★

Raise awareness of critical challenges such as reduced life expectancy, infant mortality, and low school attendance

★

Support early learning and family engagement through accessible information

★

Track progress through clear, comparative data from the 2016 and 2021 Censuses in health and education
